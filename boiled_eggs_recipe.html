How to Boil Eggs

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Salt (optional)
  • Vinegar (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Place eggs in a single layer in a saucepan or pot.
  2. Add enough water to cover the eggs by about an inch.
  3. Optional: Add a pinch of salt or a splash of vinegar to the water to prevent the eggs from cracking.
  4. Place the pan on the stove and turn the heat to high.
  5. Bring the water to a rolling boil.
  6. Once the water reaches a boil, turn off the heat and cover the pan with a lid.
  7. Let the eggs sit in the hot water for:
    • Soft-boiled: 4-6 minutes
    • Medium-boiled: 7-9 minutes
    • Hard-boiled: 10-12 minutes
  8. After the desired time, transfer the eggs to a bowl of ice water to stop the cooking process and make them easier to peel.
  9. Let the eggs cool in the ice water for about 5 minutes.
  10. Peel and enjoy your perfectly boiled eggs.
